5 Women I would like to meet

These are some women I admire. I've got to know them through their books, videos, shows, talks, and stories, but I haven't got to meet them yet.

Sometimes I imagine I get to invite them to my house one afternoon and we spend hours chatting and sharing great food and nice music. What stories would I tell them? I am more of a listener, but I bet they would ask me to share because they are just too kind...

The reasons I would like to meet these women

  • I think they are really smart and have mastered their craft
  • They always seem to be looking for ways of improving
  • They are encouragers, like to share what they know, and celebrate others
  • Their work has impacted my life in some way
  • I think I could learn so much from them
